Destination Wedding at an Affordable Destination Wedding Packages

We welcome you to turn your dreams of a destination wedding into reality. Wedding packages are always on the top of the list when planning destination weddings. Whether you want a simple destination wedding or a grand one, the place itself is so scenic that you will definitely fall in love and won’t be able to forget the experience of getting there. So, here is a little help for you with some essential things that you need to know before you book your place.

Tips for Destination Wedding

Even the average wedding has to be included with many things. Yet, you find something is missing out at the exact time you need it. If you are now planning for a destination wedding in Bali then you need to pack up some tips along with your luggage. And, here are they:

Hire a wedding planner

Hiring a team of professionals can be so helpful for you to make your big day unique. If you want your wedding to be highlighted for years to your guest and leave an unforgettable experience to then hire wedding planners like SG TRAVELLER'S who have a great Bali and other destination wedding packages and also know the ins and outs of the industry.

Find the right vendors

Now, for this, you have two options. You can find vendors on your own, if you know, who are the best in their field. Or you can leave it on the experts to hire them for you. After all, it would help if you made everything perfect.

Be honest with your budget

Of course, a destination wedding in Bali is affordable. At the same time, it’s not that cheap. So, it’s always good to look at your budget before you get into any blunder at a new place.

Expected Cost of the Wedding Package

If you wish for a decent wedding ceremony in the garden of a villa then you may need to spend $500 or less. At the same time, a great destination wedding at a mansion with hundreds of guests may need $50,000. This will include flower decorations, entertainment events, a lavish banquet for reception, fireworks, an international photographer, a monstrous cake, make-up and hair service for bride and groom, videographers, etc.

If you booked a 4-star hotel with 20-30 guests along with photographers, a dinner reception, makeup and hair services for the bride then it might ask you for around $7000-$12000 .

Whereas, if you want your wedding in a 5-star hotel with all the international standards planning , starting from the photographer’s makeup and hair service, decorations, videographers, and of course, dinner reception with some entertainment events then you’ll likely be spending around $12000-$17000 .

Who is our packages suitable for

Our packages are suitable for people who are on a bugdet and don't want to spend a lot for their wedding.
